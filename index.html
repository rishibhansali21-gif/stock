ℹ️ ABOUT GSTR-2B
GSTR-2B is a summary of invoices received from suppliers (GSTN populates this for you). You use it to:
• Match purchases: Verify supplier invoices match your records.
• Claim ITC: Ensure all input credits are documented.
• Audit: Find discrepancies with your purchase ledger.
This tool shows a mock view of your purchases as they would appear in GSTR-2B.
